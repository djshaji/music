Rock and Roll

Can Never Die !

Welcome to my Studio

I've been making music for more than a decade now. This is home for my music, and I hope you like it. My Genre is essentially Rock and Roll and Brit Pop, but I love to experiment. I have recorded 2 full length albums, and there are a bunch of studio sessions here as well. I am currently working on my third album. I am also spending the lockdown in my home studio, so a bunch of sessions I call Studio Sessions 2020 are also here, and are updated as and when I record anything.

Albums

Here is my music catalogue, which includes two complete studio albums, and a few miscelleneous tracks. I am currently recording my third album No Destination, of which some tracks have already been recorded. My latest studio sessions are also available here, which are updated automatically as I finish recording them.
